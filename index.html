PDX Portal

The Patient-Derived Xenografts (PDX) portal provides access to PDX studies in cBioPortal.

Development of this portal, including import of PDX data and the development of visualization features for PDX models is funded by the Breast Cancer Research Foundation (BCRF).

Available Studies

  • Welm PDX (unpublished 2016)
  • Endocrine-Therapy-Resistant ESR1 Variants Revealed by Genomic Characterization of Breast-Cancer-Derived Xenografts (Cell Rep 2013)
  • Breast Cancer Patient Xenografts (British Columbia, Nature 2014)
